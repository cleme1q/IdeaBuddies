First app

Hangover Buddy

Hangover Buddy helps someone who is drinking alcohol remember best practices that may reduce the chance or severity of a hangover.

  • Hydration reminders
  • Pacing prompts
  • Food and water check-ins
  • Simple, responsible drinking guidance

Hangover Buddy does not make alcohol use safe or prevent hangovers. It provides reminders for better choices.
