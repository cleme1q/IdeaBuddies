Hangover Buddy

Timely reminders that help adults make more responsible choices while drinking alcohol.

Install the app See how it helps

Support in the moment

Better choices are easier with the right reminder.

Hangover Buddy is designed for adults who choose to drink alcohol. It provides practical prompts for hydration, pacing, food, and other choices that may reduce the chance or severity of a hangover.

Core features

Simple guidance, delivered when it is useful

Hydration reminders

Prompts to drink water during the evening rather than waiting until afterward.

Pacing prompts

Timely check-ins that encourage more deliberate decisions about the next drink.

Food check-ins

Reminders to consider eating before or during alcohol consumption.

Responsible guidance

Clear information presented without judgment, pressure, or unnecessary complexity.

Important

No app can make drinking alcohol safe.

Hangover Buddy does not prevent intoxication, alcohol poisoning, impaired driving, injury, or hangovers. It is not medical advice and is not a substitute for moderation, planning, or professional care. Never drive after drinking.
