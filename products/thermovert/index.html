Recipes without the rework

Use the recipes you already find online.

Thermovert removes the manual work of adapting a conventional recipe for a Thermomix. Give it a recipe page and it organizes the ingredients and instructions, converts the cooking steps, and prepares a version that can be sent to Cookidoo.

How it works

From a website to your Thermomix

Thermovert handles the conversion path from the original recipe through a guided Thermomix-ready result.

01

Share the recipe

Paste or share a recipe from almost any supported website into Thermovert.

02

Convert the method

Thermovert interprets the ingredients and directions, then adapts them into appropriate Thermomix steps.

03

Review the result

Check the converted recipe, including quantities, timing, temperature, speed and other configuration.

04

Send it to Cookidoo

Transfer the completed recipe so it is available with guided instructions on your Thermomix.

Core features

Conversion with the configuration already included

Website recipe import

Extracts the useful recipe content from a wide range of cooking websites.

Thermomix adaptation

Reworks conventional instructions into steps appropriate for Thermomix preparation.

Guided configuration

Includes relevant time, temperature, speed, direction and mode settings within the converted steps.

Cookidoo transfer

Prepares the converted recipe for Cookidoo so it can be accessed and followed on the Thermomix.

Designed for flexibility

Keep the recipe. Change the workflow.

Thermovert is built for people who find recipes across the web but want the guided cooking experience of a Thermomix. The original recipe remains recognizable while the preparation is reorganized for the appliance.
